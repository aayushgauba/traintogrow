Clone Farming (1 hour)

Everything you need to know to grow your own cannabis plants. Beginners will learn to start plants from a seed or a clone, by cutting clones off from mother plants, then place clones in a tray as easy as carrots in a farmer’s garden. You’ll learn to grow your first plant, clone it, and build their own tray of top quality marijuana plants, plus secrets of the trade like carrot-topping to help your clones take root.

Transplanting (1 hour)

Transplanting clones into cups, buckets, and eventually into the bud room. Each step in the growing process – from seedling/cloning, to vegetation, to bud - is covered in detail to ensure you wind up with healthy, high quality marijuana buds.

Safety (30 minutes)

Learn to prevent mold, mildew, and spider mites! We teach you all the best practices and secret techniques to ensure that nothing sabotages your grow room.

Most importantly, we teach you to do all of these steps without breaking the law. Our mission is to train individuals to grow the highest quality marijuana for personal use, within the law

Cropping: It’s time for the payoff! The cropping class teaches you to cut a plant down, harvest the bud, plus bubbling techniques to create the best of the best finished product; ready to roll.

Train to Rolls: Enjoy the fruits of your labor. We train you the best way to break up, roll, and smoke weed in a pen, pipe, or paper – however you want to enjoy it.
